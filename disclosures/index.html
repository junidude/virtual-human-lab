Library Papers Notes

Disclosures

What is public, what stays private

VHL publishes enough information for readers to understand how the group is organized. It keeps signatures, original minutes, account data, personal information, protected research information, and institutional approval records private.

Affiliation notice

Affiliations identify people. They do not create institutional sponsorship, endorsement, responsibility, or resource support unless a written agreement says so.

Public and private

What we publish, and what we keep private.

Public

Public record

Name, purpose, non-commercial academic status, Representative role with consent, General Assembly structure, voting principles, membership categories, continuity, non-distribution finance, research-integrity principles, dissolution rules, and bylaws version.

Optional

Consent-based public items

Regular Member names, Advisor names, professional affiliations, annual activity summaries, annual finance summaries, redacted General Assembly resolutions, office location at country or city level, and conflict disclosures required by projects.

Private

Internal records

Signed bylaws, full meeting minutes, individual votes, personal addresses, phone numbers, private emails, identity documents, Representative acceptance documents, member registers, institution approval letters, account information, receipts, and payment records.

Never submit by email

Protected research and security material

Patient information, military or government information, controlled-access data, unpublished institutional or company information, access keys, passwords, server secrets, non-public datasets, and confidential contract terms should not be sent through public contact channels.

Support and conflicts

Support must not control the work.

Financial or in-kind support may include cloud credits, GPU access, equipment, data access, or research resources, but only under documented terms that protect scientific independence and compliance obligations.

Accepted only if documented

Money and in-kind support

Terms, provider, allowed use, data access, publication limits, ownership, and conflict implications must be documented before material support is accepted.

No control

Authorship and publication remain scholarly

Support does not confer control over authorship, publication, clinical decisions, institutional representation, research conclusions, or access to restricted data.

Compliance

Members' external duties still apply

Members must comply with employment, hospital, university, company, public-service, military, research-ethics, data-use, privacy, and intellectual-property obligations that apply to them.

Privacy notice

Email contact only for now.

VHL currently uses email contact rather than a membership form. If a form is added, this notice should be updated to match the actual fields, processors, retention period, analytics, and international transfer structure.

Information that may be processed

Depending on the inquiry, VHL may process a name or professional display name, email address, professional or academic background voluntarily provided by the sender, research interests, proposed contribution, message content, and consent records needed for governance if the person joins.

Purpose

Inquiry information is used to review and respond to the inquiry, assess a proposed membership or collaboration, maintain necessary governance and consent records if the person joins, and protect the website and contact channel from misuse. VHL does not sell inquiry information or use it for unrelated advertising.

Deletion and records

Inactive general inquiries should be deleted after a defined retention period unless a shorter deletion request is accepted or longer retention is needed for governance, legal, contract, research-compliance, or financial records.

Quick reference

Record handling at a glance.

Record Public handling Reason
Bylaws Publish redacted current public version Shows governance rules while excluding signatures and addresses
General Assembly minutes Keep originals private; publish redacted resolution summary if useful Protects individual votes, statements, signatures, and personal data
Member register Private unless the individual consents to a public profile Membership records contain personal information and governance status
Finance records Publish only category-level annual summaries when needed Account numbers, receipts, and payment information are not public records
Research support and conflicts Disclose at project, paper, or annual-summary level where required Supports research integrity without breaching confidential terms
